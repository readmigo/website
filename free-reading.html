Great literature has shaped every culture on Earth. Yet access to it has always depended on geography, income, or circumstance.

We believe that shouldn't be the case. The works of Shakespeare, Tolstoy, Austen, and thousands more have outlasted empires — they belong to everyone.

We want to lower the barrier to learning. Not with a free trial that expires, not with a limited preview. Just open the app and read.
